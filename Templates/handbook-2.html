THE PREMISE

Fifty years ago, a portal to another world opened and allowed passage of otherworldly creatures to cross into ours. Our world was entering a new age of magic; and with it, brought forth new supernatural residents to settle in our world from a mythical, alternate dimension known as Avalon.

Our story is set within one of various Special Administrative Zones known as S.A.Z. Eden: an artificial city created for humans and fae folk to coexist with one another but theres something afoot to threaten the fragile peace and stability within this city.


THE STORY SO FAR

Following the aftermath of an assault on Eden's networks & communications led by the terrorist group, Spectre, the city is yet again plunged into chaos when the city is plagued with temporal disruptions and fluctuations. These time-space glitches are starting to slowly consume Eden from the inside-out, posing a threat to its citizens with effects feared to be irreversible if not stopped soon.

Clocktower CEO Julius Atkascha confides that in the prior attack, an important relic was stolen from his possession: a powerful grimoire known as the Codex capable of creating such rifts that are happening in Eden. Thus, he and a seasoned group of professionals set off to quell the disruptions and to find their next clue. A clue, that will hopefully bring them closer to the culprit and perpetrator of these crimes...
